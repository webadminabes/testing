Orbits

About

Orbits is a strategic consulting initiative in the domain of industrial consulting and is dedicated to cater its clients by providing state-of-art solutions that fit their requirement and help them accelerate their growth. During our voyage, having served prestigious clients from a wide spectrum of product designing and manufacturing domain to process consulting, we are recognized as a premier consulting initiative by industry for providing optimized research based technical solutions to the industrial challenges. We firmly believe that to succeed, an organization must blend its digital and human capabilities and bring deep functional and domain expertise along with a range of innovative perspectives to spark change through leading edge consulting.

Vision

To become a leader in consulting domain for service and production industries by providing optimized and customized solutions.

Mission

1. Upgrading technical and behavioral skills of personnel through specialized, customized and high end training programs.
2. In-house development and commercialization of range of mechatronics and IT products for industrial and domestic applications.
3. Excelling in development of need based customized mechatronics and IT products for industrial applications.
4. Suggesting and implementing optimized solutions for process designing and improvement.
5. Preparing Individuals for state-of-art technology to be the part of competitive projects of national and international repute for grabbing relevant opportunities.
