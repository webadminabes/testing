Trainings at ABES

Outshine your Interviews

ABES Engineering College is committed to nurturing the academic and professional growth of its students. In line with this commitment, we offer a wide range of training programs that are designed to enhanced student's technical skills and overall personality development. These programs primarily encompass the Soft Skills and Technical Training, which are integral components of our educational approach.
"All trainings have been curated to make sure they have a direct impact on employability"
Objectives
  • Align academic knowledge with industry demands to enhance employability.
  • Strengthen students' grasp of technical subjects.
  • Equip students to tackle emerging technological challenges.
  • Prepare students for careers in globally recognized companies.

Student Employability Enhancement Program
  • Quantitative Aptitude: Strengthen mathematical foundations and problem-solving speed
  • Logical Reasoning: Sharpen analytical thinking and pattern recognition
  • Verbal Reasoning & Communication: Improve comprehension and professional communication
  • Mock Test & Analysis: Build test-taking stamina and time management
  • Interview preparation: Ready for campus placement and confidence improvement program
Placement Preparation
  • Campus Readiness Program (CRP): Holistic development of final-year students to make them industry-ready with soft skills, technical skills and QUAL (Quant, Apri, Logical, Reasoning)
  • Continuous Company-Specific Assessments: Simulate real placement tests & interviews of targeted mass recruiting and product bases companies such as TCS, Infosys, Capgemini, Accenture, Cognizant, Deloitte, Amazon, Atlassian S/w and CISCO etc
