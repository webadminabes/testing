About AAA

Founded in 2007 and registered with the Registrar of Societies, Meerut, the organization is working with objective of promoting and fostering multi-way communication and networking amongst Alma matter, Alumni & existing students. As on date the association has 15K members registered with it. Students of the institute are becoming members & shooting highest the gravity of the association as they passout. The association works with a general body which holds AGM & elects an Executive Council with 7 office bearers & upto 20 members on the day of Alumni-Meet-cumDecadal Celebration. On the very day alumni are felicitated for their achievements in their respective fields. The effort of connecting the alumni with the association is carried out at the grass root level by nominating departmental faculty coordinators. The alumni playing pivotal role for driving the association also join hands in mentoring, guiding and sharping the skills of students through expert lectures, seminars & workshops to pave their way in industry and international academic institutes. There has been immense contribution of the alumni towards career enhancement of ABESEC students.

Working Areas

1. Regular interaction to provide them insight about what industry expects from its employees, thus empowering them to acquire industry specific trainings and develop right aptitude and technical competence.

2. Lectures/ Seminars/Workshops on latest technologies.

3. Seminars to guide students for admission / studying abroad.

4. AAA has also introduced Mentor Mentee scheme to provide career guidance to current students from Alumni.

AAA Vision

To leverage the experience, exposure, capability and dedication of all alumni to commit and attract them to actively participate, promote, advocate and showcase alumni association as a partner of ABES so as to create a lifelong connectivity between all stakeholders of ABES and its alumni by building a strongly cemented relationship.

Mission

1. To develop and strengthen ties between Alumni and ABES by providing diverse tangible benefits including career services, networking opportunities, special events, lectures and inspire the students.

2. To foster, maintain, support, and establish a lifelong mutually beneficial bonding between ABES students and its alumni.

3. To facilitate opportunities like internship, jobs, and references for students through their vast exposure and enriched experience.

4. To share with our students and faculty members the latest innovations and technological needs in engineering and marketing from specialists for the benefit of the overall community.

Initiatives

Decadal Celebration

The Alumni Meet and Decadal Celebration, held on December 25, 2022, was a resounding success, with over 400 alumni and their family members in attendance. The event featured captivating cultural performances by the students of the Dramatic Club and Cultural Club, evoking nostalgia among the alumni as they reminisced about their days at their Alma Mater.

Alumni Day

The ABES Alumni Association takes great pride in fostering a sense of community and camaraderie among its former students. Each year, the association organizes the highly anticipated Alumni Day, in which alumni from different domains are invited for discussion and interaction with the current students in different domains and specializations.

Salaah

Salaah is a mentor community that aims to connect present students with their alumni to get guidance about the career they want to pursue further. They guide students in many fields apart from our academics to mapped with industry phase. It would be a significant help to the students to get guidance about what to do, when to do it and how to do it.

Talk Shows

The objective of talk shows is to enhance the interaction of alumni with different departments. The talk sessions involve the incredible journey of students from campus to corporates. Industry requires emerging technologies delivered in blended mode for each core and non-core branch students.

Sponsorship for our Institute Annual Fest

As alumni of esteemed institution ABES, we take great pride in being able to give back to our college community and enhance the overall college experience for the current students. We provided financial assistance to help cover the expenses of annual technical and cultural fests of our college. This support allowed the fest committee to plan and execute high quality events.

Seed Policy Support

The Abes Alumni Association adheres to the institute seed policy. Seed Policy works for eligible students from financially weaker sections.
