Message from the Advisor

At ABES Engineering College, practical learning meets academic excellence

Raghunandan Kansal

Advisor, ABESEC

Welcome to ABES Engineering College, where we believe in learning by doing. Our approach to education emphasizes the practical aspects of engineering, ensuring that students gain handson experience and real-world skills that prepare them for successful careers.

At ABES, we provide state-of-the-art laboratories, industry partnerships, and project-based learning opportunities that go beyond textbooks. Our students work on cutting-edge projects, collaborate with industry leaders, and engage in internships that offer invaluable insights into the engineering profession.

Our commitment is not just to produce engineers, but to foster innovators and problem-solvers who can make a real impact. Our esteemed faculty, many of whom bring extensive industry experience, are dedicated to guiding and mentoring students, helping them bridge the gap between theory and practice.

With us, students embark on an exciting journey where their ideas are transformed into reality, and their skills are sharpened to perfection.

Join us at ABES Engineering College, where practical learning meets academic excellence, and let's build a brighter future together.
