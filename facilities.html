Classrooms

Comprehensive range
ABES Engineering College provides a magniﬁcent and comprehensive range of facilities with in the campus for its students. The classrooms at ABESEC are contemporary, equipped with ceiling mounted LCD projectors. The college frequently organizes National & International level Conferences and Seminars on various themes and disciplines to promote industry interaction for its students.
State-of-the-art
The auditorium at ABESEC is up to date with state-of-the-art multimedia systems which can be used for presentations, seminars,fests and other academic events. The library is well equipped with avast number of books, journals, magazines and newspapers for students. The development of state-of-the-art infrastructure and technical facilities has been a continuous process at ABES Engineering College.
Endless Opportunities
The laboratories provide practical exposure to various subjects and give an opportunity to the students to experiment with what they have learnt in classrooms.
