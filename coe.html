Empowering Students with Industry-Ready Skills

At ABES Engineering College, our Center of Excellence (COE) offers industrial certification courses that boost employability and secure top placements. Focusing on practical learning aligned with international standards, students gain hands-on experience and stay updated through webinars with industry experts. Partnerships with CISCO, CompTIA, and EC-Council enhance technical and professional skills. Our successful alums in top organizations attest to the COE's impact, shaping your future with industry-ready skills and personal development.

Our Centers of Excellence

Cyber Security & Networking

Established in 2014, the Center of Excellence (COE) for Cyber Security & Networking at ABES Engineering College, in partnership with CISCO Networking Academy, has trained over 2500 students in courses like CCNA, CEH, and CyberOps Associate.

With industry partnerships including CISCO, CompTIA, and EC- Council, our COE offers diverse certification courses that enhance employability and provide opportunities for global certifications. Join our COE to gain the skills needed for high-paying jobs in cybersecurity and networking.

Robotics Process Automation

Established in 2019, the Center of Excellence (COE) for Robotics Process Automation at ABES Engineering College, in partnership with UiPath, equips students with in-demand RPA skills. Offering free UiPath RPA training and certification, students learn to develop Bots and use SLDC on projects.

This training enhances their employability and prepares them for automated business processes, leading to successful placements and sustainable careers in the industry.

Mobile & Web Application

Established in 2019, the Center of Excellence (COE) for Mobile and Web Application at ABES Engineering College equips students with skills in developing web and mobile applications.

The COE covers web technologies like JavaScript and HTML, focusing on dynamic and static applications and mobile app development for Android and iOS. Our program ensures students are proficient in the latest industry trends and technologies, preparing them for careers in this rapidly growing field.
