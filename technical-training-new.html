Technical Training

CCPD - Tech

ABES Engineering College (ABES EC) in Ghaziabad offers a comprehensive array of technical training programs aimed at bridging the gap between academic learning and industry requirements. These programs are meticulously designed to enhance students' technical competencies, making them more adept and industry-ready.

The holistic objective of CCPD-Tech is to develop Industry Ready Students through Employability Skills Training on desired essential skills for a successful career.

Objectives
  • Align academic knowledge with industry demands to enhance employability.
  • Strengthen students' grasp of technical subjects.
  • Equip students to tackle emerging technological challenges.
  • Prepare students for careers in globally recognized companies.

Overview of the services of CCPD Tech

Full Stack Development MERN Training

  • Develop Back-End using NodeJS and ExpressJS
  • Database Management with MongoDB
  • Full-Stack Integration & API Handling
  • Deployment & DevOps
  • Project-Based Learning & Industry Readiness
Data Structures & Algorithms Training
  • Strengthen Problem-Solving Skills

  • Understand Algorithmic Paradigms
  • Optimize Code for Efficiency
  • Enhance Competitive Coding
  • Implement Real-World Applications
