About Kalakrit

The Cultural Club of ABES is a vibrant club of our college known for its exceptional events in the domains of Dance, Singing & Fashion. With a cultural tradition & high spirited artistic performances, we aim to ignite the creative spirit within our engineering community. Students who have engaged in our programs receive a comprehensive experience that enhances their professional and personal development in addition to their engineering education.

We are renowned for our victories in multiple inter-college events, showcasing the remarkable talent and dedication of our members. The cultural club offers a range of events, workshops, and team-building guidance to enhance members' skills, and organizes various activities to inspire creativity and foster community engagement.

Projects

Diwali Delight

During the festive occasion of Diwali, Kalakrit: The Cultural Club of ABES carried out a compassionate gesture by delivering food and snacks to impoverished youngsters in slum regions through its "Humans of ABES" (HOA) association. The goal of this effort was to spread the joy and warmth of Diwali to everyone, even those without the financial means. By visiting several slums, the club members gave the children tasty treats and made them happy, emulating the real spirit of the festival of lights.

How To Join

Kalakrit holds a recruitment drive every year for all those enthusiastic students who wish to enhance their overall personality and take part in events & conduct them, which would further involve an upgradation of their intellect & personality. A form is floated during the recruitment drive, submitting which they would be called for a Personal Interview to enter in the club. Our club also holds the recruitment of several groups under it, such as the Fashion group, music group & dance group which hold their individual recruitments.
