About Youth And Tourism

The Youth and Tourism Club of ABESEC is dedicated to raising awareness among youth about the cultural and heritage sites of India, aiming to instill a sense of pride and responsibility towards preserving the nation’s rich legacy. By participating in various activities and events, members gain a deeper understanding of India's diverse cultural heritage, from ancient monuments and historic landmarks to traditional arts and local customs.

Highlighted event

Celebrating India

The Youth and Tourism Club of ABESEC recently hosted a vibrant event titled "Celebrating India," showcasing the rich cultural diversity of our nation. Students enthusiastically participated by donning traditional attire from various states, representing the unique cultures of India through song, dance, and open-mic performances.

The event featured an array of performances where students sang folk songs, performed traditional dances, and shared stories and poems during the open-mic session, highlighting the distinct cultural heritage of each state. This celebration not only provided an immersive cultural experience but also fostered a sense of unity and appreciation for India's diverse traditions among the attendees.

How To Join

Students must meet certain criteria: they need to maintain a minimum of 75% attendance in their classes, exhibit a curiosity for tourism, and have strong social and communication skills.

Additionally, prospective members should be interactive and cooperative, willing to engage with others and actively contribute to the club’s activities and goals.
