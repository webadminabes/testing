National Board of Accreditation

National Board of Accreditation (NBA) is an autonomous accrediting body in India, which deals with the accreditation of engineering and various technical institutions based on the quality of education being offered. NBA represents India in the Washington Accord (WA) which is an international agreement between bodies responsible for accrediting engineering degree programmes. The NBA accredited institutions signifies adherence to high standards of academic quality and infrastructure ensuring outcome based education.
