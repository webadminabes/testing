About MUN

We aim to provide a unique opportunity to engage students in a professional, friendly and collaborative environment to develop their research, communication and leadership skills. The conferences are an attempt to create a dialogue on the challenges that the world faces at present, an endeavour to put students into the framework of an international organisation and therefore sum up plausible and possible ways and means to successfully combat challenges. This will, in turn, increase their experience and knowledge of broad-based world issues and concerns, and reinforce the message of personal responsibility in this world.

Highlighted event

MUN 2021

Model United Nations, also known as Model UN or MUN, is an educational simulation and/or academic activity in which students can learn about diplomacy, international relations, and the United Nations. In MUN'21three Committees were simulated in our conference with different number of delegates participating in each of them.

Participants in the conference, known as delegates, were placed in committees and were assigned countries to represent, or occasionally other organizations or political figures, where they represented members of that body. They presented their assignments in advance, along with a topic or topics that their committee discussed. At the end of the conference, the best-performing delegates from each committee, were recognised with awards and Cash Prizes.

How To Join

Every year, in the month of June, announcements of the recruitment process are made through various channels like posters, social media, announcements in relevant classes or events and an online form is floated for the interested students.

Then, the auditions are conducted for interested students and the selected ones proceed to the interview round which is taken by the members of the current team. Based on the audition round and interview round, the potential members are selected.
