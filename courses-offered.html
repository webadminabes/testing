Through Counseling

85% seats of total approved intake will be filled through Counseling based on the JEE-2025 and eligibility notified by AICTE/AKTU for the session 2025-26.

Procedure

1

For participating in the counseling, the candidates are required to visit official website of the university www.aktu.ac.in regularly for detailed information which will be announced shortly.

2

After the counseling the candidate will get an Allotment letter.

3

Thereafter the candidate is required to visit the alloted college on the dates as mentioned in the Allotment letter for the final reporting along with the required fees and documents.

4

Please click the below mentioned link for the detailed information of the Fee structure (Academic & hostel) and required documents. https://www.abes.ac.in/courses-offered.html

5

Before coming for the reporting to the college kindly visit the college website www.abes.ac.in to know the detailed information.

Direct Admission

Learn More

15% seats will be offered to eligible candidate through direct admissions based on the guidelines of AICTE/AKTU and as per the college policy for the session 2025-26.

Procedure

1

Candidate has to apply online through the college website
https://abes.ac.in/courses-offered.php

2

Institute will shortlist the candidates on the basis of their Aggregate in qualifying exam and JEE Score/Rank.

3

Thereafter, candidate will be called for an interview.

4

On the basis of marks scored in the test and Interview. The candidate will be offered seat for the admission.

Financial Assistance

The Gate Qualified candidate having valid GATE score shall be eligible to get the financial assistance under PG scholarship scheme given by AICTE/MHRD.

1

The Non-qualified candidate, admitted through direct mode will get financial assistance/stipend of Rs. 7,000 in 1st year and 8,000 for 2nd year as per the college policy and based on selection at college level.

2

Provide financial support to publish original research work in Journal and international conference proceedings of repute.
