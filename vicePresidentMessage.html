Message from the Director CCPD

The basic principle of transforming society is transforming Education

Mr. Sachin Goel

Director CCPD, ABESEC

My dear students and educators from across the globe, the basic principle of transforming society is by transforming Education. The light of Education can illuminate the whole world, and in this endeavor, you, our beloved students, and educators, are the torchbearers. Here at ABESEC, we are continuously putting efforts into providing a conducive environment so that all learn and grow together. In today’s world, the most important thing is to challenge oneself each day and earnestly take action to make a better version of yourself.

Over the years, ABESEC has carved out an identity for itself as an institute that believes in developing a student's overall personality into a skilled and socially responsible engineer. Academic facilities are state-of-the-art, creating a vibrant learning environment. By learning under the guidance of highly rated faculty members, students understand the nuances of the engineering domain. We take the learning further by involving industry in the learning process. Through our sector connect initiative, we aggressively pursue industry-specific technical consultancy opportunities, exposing students to live projects.

Our efforts are well recognized by government bodies and industry alike. We are an NAAC and NBA-accredited institute. In 2020, NIRF published a list of the top 250 institutes nationally.

Looking ahead, our vision is clear. We aim to be at the forefront of providing Education that meets the current and future needs of the industry. We are determined to seize every opportunity that comes our way to bridge the industry-academia gap, not just at the national level, but also on the global stage

I earnestly wish and pray that we all learn, grow, and advance together.
