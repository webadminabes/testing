Industry Partnerships & MoUs

The MoUs with industries enables strengthening of industry-academia engagement. MOUs are like contracts that define the way two organizations will work together. While MOUs are technically legally binding, consider these documents as a tool to facilitate partnership and ensure a smooth working relationship between two organizations.

The main objective of this MoU is for the student community to enhance their skills and knowledge. It also help to train the Faculties for imparting industrial exposure/ training as per the industrial requirement considering the National Occupational Standards in the concerned sector, if available.

Events & News

Webathon
The theme of the hackathon is a full stack development webathon in which some problem statements will be shared by the ScholarHat team. Based on the provided problem statements, participants in the hackathon need to ensure a well-organized and collaborative development process, effective use of Git for version control, and a smooth deployment experience. This provides an opportunity to push the boundaries of what a student can create, collaborate with brilliant minds, and build solutions that can make a real impact.
Hack-O-Verse
Hack the Future: Hack-O-Verse promises an exhilarating journey of coding and creativity. Over eight hours, participants tackle real-world frontend projects, showcasing their skills and innovation in a supportive yet competitive atmosphere.
FRONT-END HANDS-ON WORKSHOP WITH SCHOLARHAT named “Beyond the Browser
Front-End Hands-on Workshop": The workshop was conducted by the Founder of ScholarHat and Microsoft MVP, Mr. Shailendra Chauhan, who has 15+ years of experience in web development and delivering training sessions to tech giants like Wipro, IBM, Civica, etc. He provided hands-on training, practical exercises, and real-world examples to ensure maximum learning and engagement. This workshop provided the students with cutting-edge skills that are highly sought after in today's job market, offering them a competitive edge in their careers.
NAVIGATING THE FUTURE
THE RISE OF AI PROFESSIONALS WITH IFACET, IIT KANPUR: The main objective of this seminar was to provide students with an in-depth understanding of AI and its industry impact, explore various AI career paths, and receive guidance to make informed career choices. The collaboration between IFACET, IIT Kanpur, and the Ikigai School of AI provided a unique and comprehensive learning experience for the participants. Students became aware of various job opportunities and career options in the field of artificial intelligence and machine learning.
