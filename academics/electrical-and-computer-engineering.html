Events & News

Lecture cum industrial visit at Sofcon India Pvt. Ltd., Noida
The Department of EN and ELCE at ABES Engineering College, Ghaziabad, organized a lecture and industrial visit to Sofcon India Pvt. Ltd., Noida for EN-3rd Year students on April 16, 2024. The visit provided students with practical exposure to industrial automation technologies like PLC, SCADA, and VFD through hands-on sessions. Additionally, interactions with industry experts offered insights into industry-institution relationships, internship opportunities, and placement criteria, making it an enriching experience for the students.
Lecture cum industrial visit at Deki Electronics, Noida
On January 19, 2024, the EN Department organized a lecture and industrial visit to Deki Electronics, Noida, as part of the 'Industrial Automation' training for 2nd-year EN and ECE students. This visit aimed to provide industrial exposure and practical knowledge. Students learned about the operation and maintenance of film capacitors controlled by programming logic controllers, the importance of safety, how products are interfaced globally, and gained insights into the industry-institution relationship and internship and placement criteria.
Alumni interaction under the ‘Placement Readiness Series’
On April 20, 2024, the Department of EN and ELCE held an Alumni interaction under the 'Placement Readiness Series' for the 2020-2024 batch eligible for TCSNQT. Topics covered included developing a strategic approach for the company, understanding the Tata Consultancy Service interview process, effectively presenting project work and internships during interviews, and discussing the TCSNQT exam paper pattern.
Expert talk on 'IPR"
The Department of Electrical & Electronics at ABES Engineering College, Ghaziabad, successfully conducted an expert talk on "IPR" on April 30, 2024. Mrs. Somya Kaushik, Associate Vice President at ELPIS Analytix, delivered an informative and interactive session for 3rd-year EN students. The talk covered the importance of Intellectual Property Rights, including patents, trademarks, copyrights, and trade secrets, emphasizing their role in protecting innovation, fostering creativity, and encouraging economic growth.
