Events & news

Guest Lecture on Full-Stack Development using MERN
YThe Department of Computer Science and Engineering with Specialization in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (CSE-AIML) at ABES Engineering College organized a guest lecture on Full-Stack Development using MERN on 26th September, 2023. The speaker for the event was Mr. Gaurav Raj, an Education Mentor at GeeksForGeeks. This insightful lecture covered various aspects of Full-Stack Development using the MERN stack, providing valuable knowledge and practical skills to the attendees.
Capture the Function
The GeeksforGeeks Student Chapter ABESEC, in collaboration with the Department of Computer Science and Engineering - Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (CSE-AIML) at ABES Engineering College, organized an event titled "Capture the Function". This event focused on engaging participants in a series of challenges and tasks designed to test and enhance their coding and problem-solving skills in a competitive yet collaborative environment.
DevTalk - Geek's Gambit
The GeeksForGeeks Student Chapter ABESEC, in collaboration with the Department of Computer Science and Engineering – Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (CSE-AIML) at ABES Engineering College, organized "DevTalk - Geek's Gambit" on 20th May - 21st May, 2024. This event was held at the auditorium and featured a series of talks, workshops, and interactive sessions aimed at enhancing the participants' knowledge and skills in various domains of software development and engineering.
