Events & News

HackIndia-2024
India's biggest Web3 hackathon, held on 14th and 15th 2024, saw participation from MCA and B.Tech students of ABES EC, MIET College, Invertis University, Quantum University, IIMT, and others. With 320 students in 60 teams, the event was a vibrant success. The top 10 teams were selected based on project presentations, pitch, and innovation. Exciting prizes were awarded to the winners.
Short Term Course (STC)
The Department of Computer Application at ABES Engineering College, Ghaziabad, in collaboration with NITTTR Chandigarh, successfully organized an online Short Term Course (STC) on "Network Science using Python" from 13th to 17th May 2024. The course attracted 35 participants, including academicians, researchers, scholars, and students from various departments such as CSE, IT, CSDS, and CE. The five-day course featured 14 sessions spread across three sessions each day.
Alumni Interaction cum Expert Talk
The Department of Computer Applications at ABES Engineering College, Ghaziabad, conducted an online Alumni Interaction cum Expert Talk on "IT Infrastructure Administration" on 4 May 2024. Part of a series, this event was attended by all students and faculty members of the department.
Tech Quest: Guest Lecture Series
Guest Lecture Series held its fourth lecture, "Prompt Engineering the Future of AI Development," for the MCA Department at ABES Engineering College, Ghaziabad. The session enhanced students' knowledge of AI opportunities, linking their prior technical understanding with emerging AI technologies. Key topics included prompt engineering, generative AI, language models, LLM foundations, space efficiency, responsible AI, Azure Open AI Service, and zero-shot prompting.
