Training Roadmap

1st Sem / 2nd Sem
Problem Solving I

Skill Set : C Language

No. of Hours : 30

Outcome : Competitive Coding in C-language

3rd Sem
AutoCAD Training

Skill Set : 2D and 3D drawings

No. of Hours : 30

Outcome : Create and modify, 2D and 3D Drafting and Design

4th Sem
SolidWorks Training I

Skill Set : CAD Animation

No. of Hours : 30

Outcome : Design and Modelling, Assemblies, and Drawings

5th Sem
SolidWorks Training II

Skill Set : CAD Animation

No. of Hours : 30

Outcome : Design, Analyse, and Visualize Complex 3D Models, Assemblies, and Drawings

Automobile Training

Skill Set : Design and analyse automotive components

No. of Hours : 30

Outcome : Exposure to Modern Vehicle Technologies, Advance Diagnostic Techniques

6th Sem
ANSYS Training

Skill Set : Design simulation of mechanical components

No. of Hours : 30

Outcome : Design and Analysing the Products under various Loading Condition.

7th Sem
CNC Training

Skill Set : CNC part programming

No. of Hours : 30

Outcome : Set, Simulate, and Perform various Operations on CNC

Full year academic fee scholarship to two 3rd year students
Groz Tools Engg. (P) Ltd., a leading OEM of industrial and heavy-duty tools, has generously awarded full-tuition fee scholarship for one year to two of our exceptional third-year Mechanical Engineering students, Mr. Amandeep Sharma and Mr. Jitesh Kumar. This scholarship was granted under the company's merit scholarship program. Mr. Ajay Rawat, GM Quality, and Mr. Himanshu Shankar, Corporate HRBP, from Groz Tools visited our campus on January 03, 2025 to present the awards to these deserving students.
